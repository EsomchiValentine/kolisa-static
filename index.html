FAQ

Got questions? We’ve got answers.

What is Kolisa Microfinance Bank?

Kolisa Microfinance Bank is a financial institution dedicated to providing accessible financial services to individuals and small businesses. We offer a range of products including micro-loans, savings accounts, and financial education to help our clients achieve their financial goals..


How can I open a savings account with Kolisa?

Opening a savings account with Kolisa is easy. You can start the process online, through our mobile app, or by visiting a branch. You'll need to provide a valid ID, proof of address, and an initial deposit. We offer different types of savings accounts to suit various financial goals.

What makes Kolisa different from traditional banks?

Kolisa focuses on serving individuals and small businesses who may not have easy access to traditional banking services. We offer more flexible loan terms, personalized service, and a strong community focus. Our goal is to foster financial inclusion and support local economic growth.


Does Kolisa offer mobile banking services?

Yes, Kolisa is committed to providing convenient banking solutions. Our mobile banking app allows you to manage your accounts, check balances, transfer funds, pay bills, and even deposit checks using your smartphone. The app is available for both Android and iOS devices, making it easy for you to bank on-the-go, 24/7. We also offer SMS banking for those who prefer text-based services. Our digital platforms are secure and user-friendly, designed to give you full control over your finances from the palm of your hand.
